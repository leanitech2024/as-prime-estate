Meet Our Founder

Dr. Lakshmi Prabhudev Jangamshetti

Founder | Visionary Leader | Healthcare Executive | Entrepreneur | Real Estate Advisor

Dr. Lakshmi Prabhudev Jangamshetti is a distinguished healthcare executive, technology leader, entrepreneur, and philanthropist based in Karnataka. With extensive leadership experience across healthcare, technology, and business, she is committed to excellence, innovation, and creating a positive social impact.

She serves as the Director of PGH Hospitals Private Limited, where she earned recognition for her outstanding leadership and management during the COVID-19 pandemic. She is also the President of Unibrain Softech Pvt. Ltd., driving innovation and growth in the technology sector.

In addition, Dr. Jangamshetti is associated as a Director with several organizations, including Airconsult Softech and Aravali Softech Services, reflecting her diverse expertise in corporate leadership and strategic business development.

As the Founder of AS Prime Estates, her vision is to provide honest, transparent, and customer-focused real estate services. She believes that every family deserves a home that offers comfort, security, and long-term value. Under her leadership, AS Prime Estates is dedicated to helping clients make informed property decisions through professional guidance and personalized support.

Beyond her professional achievements, Dr. Jangamshetti is actively involved in social service initiatives, contributing to community development and empowering people through healthcare, education, and welfare activities.
