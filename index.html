Meet Our Founder

Dr. Lakshmi Prabhudev Jangamshetti is a distinguished healthcare executive, technology leader, entrepreneur, and philanthropist based in Karnataka. With extensive leadership experience across healthcare, technology, and business, she is committed to excellence, innovation, and creating a positive social impact. She serves as the Director of PGH Hospitals Private Limited, where she earned recognition for her outstanding leadership and management during the COVID-19 pandemic. She is also the President of Unibrain Softech Pvt. Ltd., driving innovation and growth in the technology sector. As the Founder of AS Prime Estates, her vision is to provide honest, transparent, and customer-focused real estate services. She believes that every family deserves a home that offers comfort, security, and long-term value.
